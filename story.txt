For centuries Codemia village has been secluded from the outside world in the mountains. The only way out is a valley that is blocked by a mysterious castle. No one remembers it's origin, the legend says it's possesed by evil forces. Every now and then, a brave man tries to solve this mystery, but no one ever comes back... Your father shared this fate 10 years ago when you were a kid. Since then you were training vigorously in preparation for the quest to find him someday. Your adventure starts today...
